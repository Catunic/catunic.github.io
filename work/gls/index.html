GLS

Redefining parcel delivery, tracking and send design – delivering satisfaction straight to your doorstep

Challenge

Revamping the GLS project involved the pivotal task of redesigning the user experience and user interface for an international parcel delivery service. The challenge was to bring a modern touch to the service while preserving the brand identity.

My responsibilities included introducing new sections, simplifying the design system, and ensuring a consistent and intuitive design throughout the platform.

GLS is an ideal and reliable assistant for sending parcels.

Approach

I worked closely with the brand team to infuse modern elements into the design without compromising the essence of the brand. The introduction of new sections aimed to enhance the overall user experience, making the service more streamlined and intuitive. The focus was particularly on redesigning the dashboard and refining the tracking of parcel delivery statistics. Additionally, a dedicated calculator for parcel packaging materials was developed to further enrich the user's interaction with the platform.

Visuals and Design Principles

The visual redesign for GLS was driven by a dual commitment to modernization and brand consistency. The dashboard underwent significant improvements to provide users with a clear and concise overview of their parcel delivery journey. The tracking of parcel delivery statistics was refined for enhanced visibility and engagement.

Results

The outcome of the GLS project was a well-received and modernized parcel delivery service. The streamlined design, particularly on the dashboard and parcel tracking sections, contributed to a more user-friendly experience. The introduction of the parcel packaging materials calculator added practical functionality, further enhancing the overall service.

GLS now stands as a testament to how thoughtful design enhancements can elevate customer satisfaction and interaction within the realm of international parcel delivery services.
