Design Home

Landing page where minimalism meets unmatched style, studio crafting unique interiors.

Challenge

Embarking on the Design Home project introduced, I challenge of establishing a distinctive identity for an interior design studio in Russia. The primary goal was to develop landing page for design studio create individual design projects for residential, public, and commercial spaces, all while embodying the modern minimal White Nature style.

Crafting an interface that seamlessly communicates complex design information in a clear, conveying emotions of trust, professionalism, and exclusivity, was the central challenge.

Develops individual design projects for residential, public and commercial premises in a modern minimal style.

Color palette of soft neutrals

High-end typography

Approach

My approach involved a meticulous fusion of form and function. Immersed in the ethos of White Nature style, I prioritized simplicity and clarity in the interface design. Structured information and a aesthetic were key elements to ensure clients could swiftly comprehend the intricacies of the interior design projects.

Visuals and Design Principles

The visual experience of Design Home was meticulously crafted to reflect the essence of the brand—fresh ideas and a robust design approach. Emphasizing plants in the visual imagery, and infusing a fresh green color palette, aimed to evoke the energy of lightness and uniqueness. The incorporation of thin lines and circles added elements of softness, comfort, and sophistication.

Results

The outcome is a visually stunning and user-friendly platform that not only showcases the studio's design projects but also immerses clients in the style. Clients can navigate through the projects effortlessly, capturing the trust, professionalism, and exclusivity embedded in each design.

The clean interface design communicates the brand's commitment to fresh ideas and a robust design approach, establishing Design Home as a trustworthy and exclusive destination for clients seeking modern minimalism in their living and working spaces.
