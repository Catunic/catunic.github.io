Bio

My name is Сatrina a.k.a. Сatunic. I’m a UX/UI designer, specializing in creating digital products & experiences, brand identities, and interactions. Driven by my passion for design, business, and technology I try to learn and discover new things every day to create unique visuals and experiences.

With each project, I push my work to new horizons, always putting quality first. I love crafting effective visual solutions that delight users and help businesses grow.
